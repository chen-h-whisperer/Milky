Company Profile

Fujian Milky-way Technology Co., Ltd,Established in 1985, it is a company that focuses on high and multi-layer、HDI、COB LED、 A national high-tech enterprise specializing in the manufacturing of double-layer and single-layer circuit boards. The company is headquartered in Fujian and has a modern production base, committed to providing high-quality PCB solutions to global customers. By introducing advanced technology, we have achieved a high degree of automation and intelligence in the production process, improving efficiency and quality. At present, the company has an M3 factory covering an area of 100000 square meters and a building area of 63187 square meters, with strong production capacity and research and development capabilities. Our products are widely used in consumer electronics, communication, automotive, industrial control and other fields, with customers all over the world, including well-known enterprises such as Hitachi.
